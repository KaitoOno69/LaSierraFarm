About La Sierra Farm

La Sierra Farm is a tranquil retreat nestled in the serene mountain landscapes of Maragusan, Davao de Oro, Philippines. Our farm offers a unique blend of luxury and nature, providing guests with an unforgettable experience amidst the beauty of the inland wilderness.

At La Sierra Farm, we are dedicated to providing our guests with exceptional service and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're seeking a peaceful getaway or an adventurous escape, our farm offers something for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission at La Sierra Farm is to create a sanctuary where guests can reconnect with nature, relax, and rejuvenate their minds, bodies, and spirits. We strive to provide a memorable experience that combines luxury accommodations, breathtaking scenery, and personalized service.

Our Team

Our team at La Sierra Farm is comprised of dedicated individuals who are passionate about hospitality and committed to ensuring that every guest has a truly unforgettable experience. From our attentive staff to our skilled chefs, we work together to create a warm and inviting atmosphere for our guests.
