Activities

Embark on exciting outdoor adventures at La Sierra Farm, where every activity is an opportunity to connect with nature and create unforgettable memories.

Camping

Indulge in serene and joyful camping escapades amid the verdant mountain trails encircling La Sierra Farm, accompanied by expert local guides ensuring your relaxation and delight throughout.

Wedding Events

Celebrate your love with a one-of-a-kind wedding experience amidst the tranquil mountain trails of La Sierra Farm. Our exclusive wedding packages offer romance and adventure, guided by experienced local hosts. Relax under the stars, surrounded by nature's beauty, as you create cherished memories to last a lifetime.

Sea of Clouds Viewing

Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the sea of clouds from the majestic mountain trails surrounding La Sierra Farm. Join our guided tours led by knowledgeable local guides, and immerse yourself in the breathtaking scenery as you witness nature's marvel unfold before your eyes. Relax and unwind amidst the serene atmosphere, savoring every moment of this enchanting experience high above the clouds.

Stargazing

Embark on a mesmerizing stargazing adventure amid the serene mountain trails of La Sierra Farm. Join our expert-led tours to witness the beauty of the night sky away from city lights. Relax and marvel at the celestial wonders as you create unforgettable memories under the stars.
