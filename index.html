Enjoy The Mountain View of Maragusan

At La Sierra Farm, nestled in the serene mountain landscapes, indulge in a lavish retreat offering breathtaking vistas of the surrounding peaks. Our guests embark on an extravagant camping experience within exquisitely designed private yurts, each boasting a cozy gas fireplace, a spacious king-size bed, and a secluded deck overlooking the majestic mountains. Experience the allure of the inland wilderness while reveling in the comfort and luxury of La Sierra Farm's opulent accommodations.

  • Mountain Viewing Deck: Immerse yourself in the stunning vistas of the surrounding peaks from our exclusive mountain viewing deck, offering panoramic views of the rugged terrain
  • Group Fireplace and Stargazing: Gather around our communal fireplace for cozy evenings under the stars, sharing stories and enjoying the mesmerizing sight of the night sky
  • Complimentary Breakfast: Begin your day with a delectable cooked-to-order breakfast, on us, ensuring you're energized for your mountain adventures
  • Lodge Lounge: Unwind in our inviting lodge with complimentary appetizers and beverages, the perfect retreat after a day of exploring mountain trails
  • Nightly Fine Dining with a View: Delight in our nightly fine dining experiences, accompanied by sweeping mountain vistas, creating unforgettable dining memories under the vast night sky
